Parks

Utah has many parks that are perfect for walking and exercising your dog. These dog-friendly parks provide open spaces, trails, and relaxing environments for both dogs and their owners. Here are some popular parks you can visit with your dog.

Popular Dog-Friendly Parks in Utah

Liberty Park - Salt Lake City

Liberty Park is one of the largest and most popular parks in Salt Lake City. Dogs are welcome on leash, and the park features wide walking paths, large grassy areas, and a peaceful lake. It is a great place for dog owners to enjoy walks, picnics, and relaxing outdoor time with their pets.

Run-A-Muk Dog Park - Park City

Run-A-Muk Dog Park is a large off-leash dog park located near the Utah Olympic Park. The park includes over 40 acres of open space and trails where dogs can run freely and explore nature. The area is also known for its beautiful mountain scenery and fresh air.

Willow Creek Dog Pond - Park City

Willow Creek Dog Pond is a fenced dog park that includes a pond where dogs can swim and cool off. The park also offers benches, shaded areas, and agility equipment, making it a fun place for dogs to exercise and socialize with other pets.

Herman Franks Dog Park - Salt Lake City

Herman Franks Dog Park is a fenced off-leash park where dogs can run freely and interact with other dogs. The park includes agility equipment, seating areas for owners, and water stations to help keep pets hydrated.

Antelope Island State Park - Great Salt Lake

Antelope Island State Park offers stunning views of the Great Salt Lake and miles of scenic trails. Dogs are allowed in many areas of the park, making it a great location for hiking and exploring nature with your dog.

Tip: Always bring water, waste bags, and keep your dog supervised while visiting dog parks.
